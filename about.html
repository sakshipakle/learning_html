About Pets House

Our Mission

At Pets House, our mission is to connect every pet lover with their perfect furry, feathery, or fluffy companion, and to promote ethical pet adoption and care across the country.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every pet finds a loving home, and every home finds a loyal pet. We aim to be India's most trusted platform for pet adoption and pet care.

Why Choose Us?

  • Healthy and vaccinated pets
  • Verified and trusted sellers
  • Affordable prices
  • Post-adoption support
